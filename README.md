# AliExpress Total Spend Calculator (Working as of December 3. 2025)

This script calculates how much you have spent on AliExpress by automatically loading all your past orders and summarizing the totals.

## What It Does

- Loads all orders from your AliExpress order history page.
- Extracts product titles, prices, and purchase years.
- Detects your currency automatically.
- Displays:
  - A detailed table of all orders
  - Total spent per year
  - Total spent overall

## How to Use

1. Log in to AliExpress and open your **Order History** page.
2. Open your browser's **Developer Console**.
3. Paste the entire script and press **Enter**.

The script will load any remaining orders automatically and print your spending summary in the console.
